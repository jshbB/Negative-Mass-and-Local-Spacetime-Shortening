# Negative Mass and Local Spacetime Shortening: A Theoretical Study

**Author:** Jewel Sha Hasan  
**Date:** March 2026  

---

## Overview

This paper explores the theoretical effects of **negative mass** on spacetime curvature within the framework of General Relativity.  
It introduces the concept of **"spacetime shortening"**, where negative mass produces repulsive curvature and inverted time dilation relative to distant observers.  
Potential implications include exotic structures like wormholes and speculative warp-drive-like phenomena.
